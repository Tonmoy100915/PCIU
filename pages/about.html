About Port City International University

Port City International University (PCIU) is a private university in Bangladesh established in 2013. The university is located in Khulshi, Chattogram and is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of Bangladesh.

PCIU offers undergraduate and graduate programs in various disciplines including Business Administration, Computer Science, Engineering, Law, and Health Sciences.

The university is committed to providing quality education and fostering research and innovation among students and faculty members.

Historical Outline

2013

Port City International University was established with approval from the Government of Bangladesh and University Grants Commission (UGC).

2014

First academic session began with four departments: Business Administration, Computer Science, English, and Law.

2016

New departments added including Electrical & Electronic Engineering and Pharmacy.

2019

First convocation held with graduates from the initial batches.

2022

Construction began on new permanent campus at Bakalia, Chattogram.
